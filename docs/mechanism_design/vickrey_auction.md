# Vickrey Auction

The Vickrey auction is a type of sealed-bid auction where bidders submit written bids without knowing the bid of the other people in the auction. The highest bidder wins but the price paid is the second-highest bid.

This type of auction is strategically similar to an English auction and gives bidders an incentive to bid their true valuation of the item.

## Key Properties

- **Truthful bidding is a dominant strategy:** A bidder's dominant strategy is to bid exactly their true value for the item.
- **Efficiency:** The item is awarded to the bidder with the highest valuation.
